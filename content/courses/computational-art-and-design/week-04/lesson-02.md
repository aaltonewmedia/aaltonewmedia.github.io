## p5js Sound Library

[Sound library reference](https://p5js.org/reference/#/libraries/p5.sound)

The p5.js sound library allows you to:
- load, play and manipulate sound files
- synthesize sounds
- use audio filters
- get audio input from microphones and other input devices
- analyze sound
